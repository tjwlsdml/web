DESIGN의 속성

term design comes from the Latin word design , which means direct, express, and achieve. In general, because design outcomes must achieve a variety of objectives simultaneously and effectively,

design work must take into account various aspects (e.g., materials, production efficiency, safety, economics, durability, attractiveness, etc.) in the process of industrial design, which may involve design research, thinking, experimental models, interactive experiments and coordination,

and redesign processes prior to the creation process. Therefore, design as a process has many different methods and forms depending on how the object is designed,

the field of individuals and participants participating in it, and its diversity. In philosophy, design as an abstract word is referred to as a meaning with any pattern or reason for existence and purpose.
